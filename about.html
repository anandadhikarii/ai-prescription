About Our Project

The AI Prescription Verifier is an innovative system designed to assist doctors, pharmacists, and patients in analyzing prescriptions quickly and safely. Our platform uses advanced AI algorithms to:

  • ✅ Detect possible drug interactions and side effects.
  • ✅ Verify correct dosages based on patient age and conditions.
  • ✅ Suggest safe alternatives for risky medicines.
  • ✅ Provide a user-friendly interface for quick analysis.

By combining medical knowledge with AI technology, our mission is to make prescription management safer, faster, and more reliable for everyone.
